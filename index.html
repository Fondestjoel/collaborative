Why Choose Us?

01

Relational-learning approach

We design and deliver our courses with the learner at the heart of everything. This we do by employing relational learning methods that allow facilitators and learners to develop trust and mutual respect which are key ingredients necessary for quality learning.

02

Positive learning environment

With the understanding that environments play a critical role in learning, we give special attention to our learning space. Every piece of furniture and spatial design is put in a way that stimulates the learning engagement at COCRIC.

03

Professional Trainers

We pride ourselves in engaging the best brains to deliver our courses. Our facilitators are drawn from industry experts, the academia and people with the practical experience and exposure to deliver content that transform lives.

Featured Courses Upgrade your skills with our exciting course offerings

Adventure Education

This unique course is tailored towards building team skills and providing the competence needed to facilitate outdoor based learning. It involves learning through challenges, risk-taking and teamwork. Participants learn from each other and acquire capabilities that find application to life and work.

Design Thinking

This course is speficically adapted for educators and teachers. It offers the opportunity for creative collaboration when seeking solutions to challenges in your classroom, school, and community. It hones your skills and empowers you to create desirable and innovative solutions.

Staff Productivity

At COCRIC, we believe that responsive, customized, need-based training makes the difference between a good workforce and a great one. In this course, we help teachers discover key elements that result in productivity and optimum performance. We also train school administrators on how to take on greater responsibilities, manage different types of structures to setting realistic and measurable goals that improve the performance of their teams.

Digital Literacy

This course launches teachers into the core of digital skills that will enable them translate teaching and learning in the most learner-centered way using technology skills and tools.

Teacher Resources

At COCRIC we provide excellent teaching and learning resources such as software, games, charts,and supplies that support teachers in improving learning outcomes in the classroom.
